Set once, seen everywhere.

Set your photo, name, and other profile details just once, and your Gravatar will follow you everywhere you go, bringing your identity along.

It starts with an email.

Your email address is your key to having a Gravatar. Just associate your email with the same account on each new service, and your profile comes with you.

Trusted by big platforms.

Millions of websites like WordPress, Stack, and Disqus use your Gravatar as an authentication token, verifying your identity across your digital footprint.
