Cookie Based Opt-Out

Some things to keep in mind when using the cookie opt-out:

  • • If you use more than one computer, browser or browser profile, you will need to opt-out of cookies on every browser you use
  • • If you clear or delete your cookies, this will result in the deletion of your Trade Desk opt-out cookie, meaning you will need to re-opt the out of our following that deletion
  • • If you are opting out, you may continue to receive advertising through our services, but those ads may be less relevant
  • • After you opt-out, your browser's Trade Desk advertising cookie will be set to a zero mapping your Trade Desk campaign cookie, and no longer allow us to target advertising to your interests based on this cookie

Please note that if you block or otherwise restrict cookies this can affect choices, such as The Trade Desk opt-out, that are linked to such cookies.
