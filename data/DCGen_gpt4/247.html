We created EepURL as a trackable shortening service for Mailchimp's built-in Twitter integration. At the time, we opened it up to the public, because we felt there werent enough monkey-based URL shorteners out there.

This service is no longer available for public use. We made this change in order to protect the reputation of the domain and high deliverability rates for Mailchimp customers who use EepURLs in their campaigns.

Existing URLs are still being redirected, and EepURL still works in the app.

If you're looking to build your own url shortener, take a moment to look at Shaun Inmans Lessn. Building your own url shortener is a good way to avoid having your links connected to a blocklisted domain name.
