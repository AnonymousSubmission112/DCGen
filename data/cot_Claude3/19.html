WHAT IS THE DIGITAL OBJECT IDENTIFIER (DOI)?

A DOI names a digital identifier of an object, not objects – physical, digital, or abstract. DOIs offer a convenient, reliable, linking tool to Share. There can be further metadata, context, or additiona.

Designed to be used by humans as well as machines, DOIs identify digital 'persistently'. This allows things to be uniquely identified and accessed reliably. You know what you have, where it is, and where you got it.
