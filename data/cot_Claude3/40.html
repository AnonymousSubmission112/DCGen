One profile for everything, everywhere.

One avatar attached to everything you do online. Control and manage your personal data with complete control over your personal data.

Set once, seen everywhere.

When you change your avatar profile, your avatar updates everywhere. No need to update your avatar in multiple places.

It starts with an email.

Your Gravatar profile is tied to your email address. When you sign up or log in with your email account, the web site knows to load your profile from Gravatar.

Trusted by big platforms.

Millions of websites like WordPress, Stack, and Discord use your Gravatar profile automatically. Whenever you use any of those services, your profile will appear.

Public, open, and responsible.

You control over the data that you share with the tools you use. You decide when to share your profile and how public or private it is. With Gravatar, your data is yours and yours only.

Use the Developer API 
<script>
    window._gravatar = window._gravatar || [];
    _gravatar.push(['profile', {
        email: 'example@example.com',
        callback: function(profile) {
            // Display user's avatar
            var avatar = profile.profileUrl;
            document.getElementById('avatar').src = avatar;
        }
    }]);
    var gravatar=document.createElement('script');
    gravatar.type='text/javascript';
    gravatar.async=true;
    gravatar.src=('https:'==document.location.protocol ?
        'https://secure' : 'http://insecure')+
        '.gravatar.com/js/gprofiles.js';
    var s=document.getElementsByTagName('script')[0];
    s.parentNode.insertBefore(gravatar, s);
</script>
