Top Features

MediaFire is more than just storing and sharing. Here's how it can be the solution to many of the issues you face.

Cloud Storage to hold all Files

Store up to 10GB of files and documents in the secure MediaFire cloud storage service free of charge. There are upgrade options available for more space.

Unlimited bandwidth & downloads

Share files of any size with unlimited bandwidth and downloads. There's no need to worry about running out of transfer bandwidth or recipients having trouble downloading large files.

Easily share with anyone

Share files and folders with anyone, anywhere. You can password protect files and set expiration dates for secure sharing. Plus, our mobile apps make sharing on the go a breeze.

Manage access at ease

Have complete control over who can access your files and folders. You can set permissions and revoke access at any time, even for files that have already been shared.

Organize with ease

Create folders and sub-folders to keep your files organized. You can even upload entire folder structures from your computer and maintain the same organization in the cloud.

More Than 1 Link

You can create more than one public link for any file or folder. This allows you to share the same content with different groups of people using separate links.

No matter where you go - take your files with you.

Access your files from any browser or from our desktop and mobile apps.
