iCloud for Developers

iCloud securely stores your users' data and seamlessly keeps it up to date across their devices, so they'll have a great experience no matter which device they use. And with powerful and intuitive tools, such as CloudKit Console, you can focus on delivering great apps while iCloud handles the details of scaling, consistency, and security.

Build apps using CloudKit

Develop apps with world-class authentication, privacy, security, and syncing using the same technologies used by Apple in its most popular cloud offerings. CloudKit provides a trusted framework and a comprehensive set of tools that make it easy for you to develop powerful apps that seamlessly sync across OS, watchOS, tvOS, iOS, and the web.

Learn about CloudKit

Gain insights with Telemetry

Better understand the experiences and motivation of your users by measuring user activity. With CloudKit database usage, and trends over time, the Telemetry's monitoring and analytics features, you can visualize aggregate behavior across all of your users' devices for all of the data coming into your iCloud databases.

Learn about Telemetry

Access logs that respect user privacy

Easily collect, process, and analyze data while keeping your users' data private. CloudKit provides detailed, real-time, and historical logs of server activity. Log streams don't contain any private or user data, so you'll have everything you need to debug and analyze your app while ensuring complete user privacy and compliance.

Learn about logs

Send, validate, and monitor push notifications

Use the infrastructure and services to send text push notifications to Apple devices through the Apple Push Notification service (APNs). You can access logs that provide insights into the delivery process and help you with monitoring and validating them. Your cloud-hosted logic can integrate the sending of push notifications to your app's workflow.

Learn about Push Notifications
